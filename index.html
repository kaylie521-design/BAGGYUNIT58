Print design, a subset of graphic design, is a form of visual communication used to convey information to an audience through intentional aesthetic design printed on a tangible surface, designed to be printed on paper, as opposed to presented on a digital platform.

Screen Printing

Screen printing is a more involved process that uses thick inks that lay on top of the t-shirt rather than having the t-shirt absorb the colors. The method involves creating a stencil on a fine mesh screen. With that, you then push the paint or ink through.
